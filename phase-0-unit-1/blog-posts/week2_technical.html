Hello guys,

In this weeks challenge I'm going to be teaching you the main difference between Margin, Borders, and Padding. And the best way of learning is by actually doing it. In the image below I made two boxes with the same width, and height.



The result is as expected, but lets change it up by adding padding: 10px to box_2.


Above you can see how padding clears an area around the content. Now lets add border: 2px solid .



As you can see the border goes around the padding and the content. Now lets look at how the final box looks after we add margin: 10px , padding, and a border.



The margin clears the area around the border.


So to wrap things up the margin is the space around the element. The larger the margin, the more space between our element and the elements around it. The border is the edge of the element, and padding is the spacing between the content and the border.
